Origin Story

Why I'm Building Muziclly

"Growing up in India, I was fortunate to have access to quality music education. But I watched countless talented friends abandon their musical dreams—not because of lack of passion, but because of lack of access.

A student in a small town shouldn't have to move to a metro city to learn guitar properly. A talented homemaker shouldn't have to give up her passion because she couldn't find students. Parents shouldn't have to choose between paying rent and paying for their child's music lessons.

Muziclly is my answer to these problems. It combines everything I know about music, teaching, and technology into a platform that can genuinely transform lives—at scale."
