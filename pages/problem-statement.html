Pain Points

Everyone Struggles in the Current System

Students

  • Can't find verified, quality teachers
  • No structured curriculum or progression path
  • Expensive one-on-one lessons
  • No recognized certification even after years
  • Isolation—no community or performance opportunities

Teachers

  • Unstable, unpredictable income
  • Endless marketing and student acquisition
  • No platform to showcase credentials
  • Time wasted on scheduling and payments
  • Limited to local geography

Parents

  • No visibility into child's actual progress
  • Difficult to assess teacher quality
  • High expense with uncertain ROI
  • No benchmarks or milestones to track
  • Safety concerns with unknown tutors
